# Teen-age-Phone-Addiction-Analysis-Dashboard-Excel-
Teenage phone addiction is the excessive and uncontrollable use of smartphones by teens, often leading to negative effects on their mental health, academics, sleep, and social life. Constant engagement with social media, games, and notifications can cause anxiety, poor focus, and reduced real-life interactions. Addressing this issue is important to promote healthier digital habits among teenagers.
